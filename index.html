About HEATConf

Welcome to the Health, Engineering, Agriculture and Technology (HEAT) Conference website. This conference is organized annualy by the Council for Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR)-Institute for Scientific and Technological Information (INSTI). CSIR-INSTI is the hub of integrated scientific and technical information (STI) in Ghana that provides the major public research and academic institutions as well as the industrial sector with resources and services designed to improve dissemination of, and access to indigenous and international STI.

The Institute serves as the main STI resources clearinghouse for CSIR. INSTIâ€™s mandate is to develop national capacity and capability for the efficient and effective delivery of real-time scientific and technological information (STI) and customized knowledge on demand for the benefit of policy makers, research scientists, industrialists, and others in appropriately packaged form for national development. The HEAT Conference therefore serves as a platform for researchers in the respective fields to showcase research work and research output.
